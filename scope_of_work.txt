SCOPE OF WORK
Online Archery Gaming Website

IN SCOPE
1. Game engine
   - Canvas render loop targeting 60 FPS using requestAnimationFrame.
   - Custom 2D physics: gravity, velocity, and horizontal wind offset in vanilla JS.

2. Bow and shooting mechanic
   - Power bar charged by holding mouse or tap.
   - Power bar fills 0% to 100% linearly over 2 seconds.
   - Power bar holds at 100%, does not cycle, and does not auto-fire.
   - Power bar resets to 0% after each shot.
   - Release fires the arrow at the current power.

3. Target and scoring
   - 5-ring circular target with distance-based hit detection.
   - Points: Bullseye 10, Gold 8, Red 6, Blue 4, Black 2; miss 0.
   - Ring radii at baseline: Bullseye 20px, Gold 40px, Red 65px, Blue 95px, Black 130px.
   - Maximum round score is 100 points.

4. Wind system
   - On-screen wind indicator.
   - Wind alters arrow trajectory using the active difficulty wind range.

5. Difficulty levels (3 total)
   - Easy:   1.0x scale,  wind +/-1 to +/-3 px per frame,  60s timer.
   - Medium: 0.7x scale,  wind +/-4 to +/-6 px per frame,  45s timer.
   - Hard:   0.45x scale, wind +/-7 to +/-12 px per frame, 30s timer.

6. Round logic
   - 10 arrows per round.
   - Round ends after the 10th arrow or when the timer reaches 0.

7. Leaderboard
   - Top 10 scores stored in localStorage.
   - Sorted in descending order.
   - Persists across page refresh.
   - Stores initials (3 characters), score, and difficulty.

8. Screens and navigation
   - Home, Game, Results, and Leaderboard screens.
   - All four screens reachable without a page reload.

9. UI theme
   - Medieval and nature theme.
   - Palette: Forest Green #2D5A27, Aged Wood #8B6914, Cream Parchment #F5E6C8,
     Deep Red #8B1A1A, Sky Blue #87CEEB.
   - Crimson Red #C0392B is used only for the target Red ring.

10. Documentation
    - README.md with setup and play instructions.

OUT OF SCOPE
- Backend, server, or database.
- Authentication or login.
- Paid libraries or plugins.
- Multiplayer.
- AI opponents.
- Unity, React, or any framework. Vanilla JS only.

DELIVERABLES
1. index.html  - main entry point.
2. style.css   - all styling.
3. game.js     - all game logic.
4. assets/     - all images and sounds used.
5. README.md   - setup and play instructions.
